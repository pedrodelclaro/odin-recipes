Garlic Oil Pasta

Description

Super easy and begginers friendly recipe of a delicious Garlic Oil Pasta. This was the first recipe my boyfriend teached me how to cook. You can add bacon if you want!

Ingredients

  • 200 grams pasta
  • 2 big garlic cloves
  • 7 tablespoons olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. In a pan with boiling water, add salt to taste and the pasta. Then cook for approximately 10 minutes.
  2. While the pasta cooks, add, in another pan, the olive oil and the chopped garlic.
  3. Cook the garlic until brown. Then, turn of the fire and add parsley and black pepper to taste.
  4. When the pasta is ready combine with the sauce and serve while hot.
