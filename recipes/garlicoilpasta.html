Garlic Oil Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200 grams pasta
  • 2 big garlic cloves
  • 7 tablespoons olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. Step 1: In a pan with boiling water, add salt to taste and the pasta. Then cook for approximately 10 minutes
  2. Step 2: While the pasta cooks, add, in another pan, the olive oil and the chopped garlic
  3. Step 3: Cook the garlic until brown. Then, turn of the fire and add parsley and black pepper to taste
  4. Step 4: When the pasta is ready combine with the sauce and serve while hot
